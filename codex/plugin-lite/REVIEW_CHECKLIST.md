# Review Checklist

Codex review must check:

1. `EVIDENCE_DIGEST.md`
2. `RESULT.md`
3. `VERIFY.md` and `verify.json`
4. `diff-summary.md`
5. `changed-files.json`
6. boundary violations and unexpected changes
7. observed usage completeness
8. open issues from Kimi

`Worker finished` does not mean accepted.

`VERIFY pass` does not mean accepted.

`accepted` requires a Codex review decision.
